Games selected for the final showcase

Run Double Jump 2018 was a tough call, it has been the year with highest number of submission since we started hosting RDJ in 2015
We had to go through a lot of submissions to choose who will be the finalists to showcase and pitch thier games for a chance to win a place to compete for the indie prize awardss the games that made it to the final showcase are:

  • Aliens vs Cowmander
  • Back to Back
  • Retro Bouncer
  • Bird View Burger Barn
  • Disco Fiasco
  • RhythmAttack-VR
  • Soul Piece
  • TechnoBox

the final judges went through the games and decided this year winner to be Aliens vs Cowmander
